4. Final Report and System (50% of 45 points = 22.5 points)
A strong report should read like a short research or workshop paper, describe and present a visual
analytics system, and clearly demonstrate how the system supports analytical reasoning and insight
generation.
If generative AI tools are used, their usage must be documented and the results must be critically
evaluated and validated. Lack of validation may result in score deductions in relevant sections.
Students are responsible for verifying correctness and appropriately using any AI-generated content.
a. Organization & Writing Quality (2 points)
2 pts
- Report is logically structured and easy to follow
- Writing is clear and concise with minimal grammatical errors
- Figures are used effectively to support explanations, system design, and findings
- Figures are properly referenced and integrated into the text
1 pt
- Some issues with clarity, organization, or writing quality
- Minor redundancy or verbosity
- Figures are present but not well used for explanations
0 pts
- Difficult to follow or significant writing issues
- Figures missing or unclear

2 pts
- Problem clearly defined and well motivated
- Strong relevance to visual analytics
- Scope is appropriate and non-trivial for a graduate project
1 pt
- Problem is clear but motivation or VA relevance is somewhat weak
- Scope may be slightly too simple or too broad
0.5 pts
- Problem unclear or weakly motivated
- Limited connection to visual analytics
0 pts
- No clear problem definition

2 pts

- Clear explanation of how prior work relates to and differs from this project
1 pt
- Some relevant papers cited but connections are weak or incomplete
0.5 pts
- Few or loosely related references
0 pts
- No meaningful related work

5.5 pts
- Approach clearly described and technically sound

- Design decisions are well justified

3.5 pts

4.5 pts
